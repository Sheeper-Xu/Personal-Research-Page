3 Featured Research

Design and Control of Integrated Multiphase Machines With Decoupled Rotors

2023 — Present

Proposed integrated machines with multiple decoupled rotors based on multiphase orthogonal subspaces, and developed their generalized topology, unified design methodology, flux-modulation enhancement, and decoupled control. Five-phase dual-rotor and seven-phase triple-rotor prototypes, a multiphase drive platform, and a coordinated test rig based on SIEMENS and RT-LAB were constructed.

Limited-Angle Vibration Motors for High-Frequency Reciprocating Actuators

2021 — 2023

Developed an electromechanical coupling model that reveals the inherent resonance characteristic, and proposed a concurrent design methodology guided by the resonance point and load condition to maximize system-level efficiency. Three prototypes were fabricated, with non-contact vibration measurement implemented using infrared sensors and a high-speed camera.

Magnetoelectric Coupling of Ammonium Salts at Low Temperature

2021 — 2023

Investigated magnetoelectric coupling driven by proton motion at low temperature and carried out the primary experimental measurements, expanding the range of multiferroic materials for information-storage technologies. Electrical polarization, magnetic properties, and low-temperature phase transitions were characterized in ammonium salts and organic–inorganic hybrid materials.
